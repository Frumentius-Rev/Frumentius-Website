Discovering Ethiopian Food

Ethiopian food is one of the most unique and flavorful cuisines in the world. It is known for its rich spices, communal style of eating, and the famous sourdough flatbread called injera.Meals are usually shared from a single platter, which brings people together and makes dining a social experience.The cuisine features a wide variety of stews known as wot, often made with lentils, vegetables,chicken, or beef.
